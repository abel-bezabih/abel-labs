## Phase 5: Revenue Optimization

### Pricing Strategy:

**Current Pricing (from requirements):**
- Landing Page: 30,000 - 50,000 ETB (~$500-850 USD)
- Full Website: 70,000 - 120,000 ETB (~$1,200-2,000 USD)
- Mobile App: 350,000 - 800,000 ETB (~$6,000-13,500 USD)
- Maintenance: 3,000 - 10,000 ETB/month (~$50-170/month)

**Revenue Projections:**
- 2 websites/month @ 100k ETB avg = 200k ETB/month (~$3,400)
- 1 mobile app/quarter @ 500k ETB = 166k ETB/month (~$2,800)
- 10 maintenance clients @ 5k ETB = 50k ETB/month (~$850)
- **Total: ~416k ETB/month (~$7,000/month) = $84k/year**

**Scaling Path:**
- Month 1-2: 2-3 projects (proof of concept)
- Month 3-4: 5-8 projects (word of mouth)
- Month 5-6: 10-15 projects (marketing kicks in)
- Year 2: 20+ projects/month + recurring maintenance
